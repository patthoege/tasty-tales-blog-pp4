Editing your post

The body of your content can be edited using the Rich Text Editor. Your post remains private until you “publish” the article.


How to use the editor

You can edit and format the main content of your article using the Rich Text Editor. Use the toolbar above the text area to apply various formatting options:

  • Headers: Select different heading levels for your text.
  • Images: Click the "Image" button to add or upload images.
  • Font Styles: Apply bold, italic, or underline to your text.
  • Lists: Create ordered or unordered lists with ease.
  • Undo/Redo: Use the undo/redo buttons to revert or reapply changes.
